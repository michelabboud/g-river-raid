## Getting Started

### Objective
Navigate your fighter jet through an endless, procedurally generated river while:
- **Destroying enemies** for points
- **Collecting fuel** to stay airborne
- **Avoiding obstacles** to survive
- **Defeating bosses** to progress through levels
- **Earning coins** to unlock features

### Starting a Game

1. **Insert Coin** - Click "Insert Coin" on the main menu
2. **Choose Wingman** (Optional) - Spend 5 Bravery Coins to activate AI companion
3. **Start Game** - Click "Start Game" to begin your mission
4. **Good Luck!** - The river awaits!

## Gameplay Mechanics

### 1. Fuel Management

**Critical System:** Your jet constantly consumes fuel. Running out of fuel = instant crash!

#### Fuel Consumption
- **Normal flight**: Steady fuel consumption
- **Accelerating**: Increased fuel consumption
- **Base rate**: ~1% per few seconds (varies by difficulty)

#### Refueling Options

**Fuel Canisters** (Pink/Red icons)
- Float in the river
- Fly over them to collect
- Restores fuel gradually (typically +20-30%)
- Most common refueling method

**Fuel Depots** (Blue structures)
- Fixed locations on riverbanks
- Instantly refill tank to 100%
- Less common than canisters
- Strategic collection points

**Fuel Management Tips:**
- Never let fuel drop below 20%
- Plan your route to hit fuel canisters
- Prioritize fuel over enemy kills when low
- Boss defeats also restore some fuel

---

### 2. Combat System

#### Shooting Mechanics
- **Default Weapon**: Single forward-firing bullet
- **Fire Rate**: Moderate (can be upgraded)
- **Bullet Speed**: Fast (can be upgraded)
- **Upgrades**: Spread shot, rapid fire, speed boost

#### Enemy Destruction
- Shoot enemies before they reach you
- Some enemies require multiple hits
- Destroyed enemies may drop power-ups or coins
- Collision with enemies = lose a life

#### Targeting Priority
1. **Immediate threats** (enemies coming at you)
2. **High-value targets** (jets, helicopters)
3. **Fuel canisters** (when fuel is low)
4. **Collectibles** (when safe to do so)

---

### 3. Navigation

#### River Terrain
- **River banks**: Instant death on collision
- **Width**: Varies throughout the course
- **Obstacles**: Islands, bridges, structures
- **Generation**: Procedurally generated for endless play

#### Movement Strategy
- **Stay centered** when possible for maximum maneuverability
- **Plan ahead** - watch for narrow passages
- **Smooth movements** - avoid jerky controls
- **Speed control** - faster = harder to maneuver

## Wingman System

### Overview
The Wingman is an AI-controlled companion drone that assists you in combat.

### Activation
- **Cost**: 5 Bravery Coins
- **Availability**: Before starting a game
- **Duration**: Entire game session
- **Toggle**: On/off selection on start screen

### Wingman Abilities

**Autonomous Combat**
- Automatically targets and shoots enemies
- Independent aiming system
- Focuses on nearest threats
- Never runs out of ammunition

**Formation Flight**
- Flies alongside your aircraft
- Maintains relative position
- Avoids obstacles
- Survives until game over

**Bonus Earnings**
- **Doubles coin generation** (2 coins/min vs 1 coin/min)
- Does NOT share kills (you still get points)
- Cost-effective for long runs

### Strategy Tips
- **Worth it for long games** (break-even at 5+ minutes)
- **Recommended for beginners** (extra firepower helps)
- **Useful in boss battles** (sustained DPS)
- **Save coins** if you're still practicing

## Boss Battles

### When Bosses Appear
- End of each major river section
- Every ~5-10 minutes of gameplay
- After destroying certain bridges
- Progressive difficulty increases

### Boss Characteristics

**High Health Pool**
- Requires many hits to destroy
- Health bar displayed on screen
- Multiple damage phases

**Attack Patterns**
- Unique to each boss
- Projectile volleys
- Movement patterns
- Increasing aggression at low health

**Environmental Hazards**
- Boss battles in confined spaces
- River still present (avoid banks!)
- Limited maneuvering room

### Boss Fight Strategy

1. **Maintain Fuel** - Ensure >50% fuel before engaging
2. **Use Power-Ups** - Save rapid fire and spread shot
3. **Pattern Recognition** - Learn attack patterns
4. **Stay Mobile** - Keep moving to dodge attacks
5. **Focus Fire** - Consistent damage is key
6. **Patience** - Don't rush, survival first

### Rewards
- **Large point bonus** (1000-5000 points)
- **Fuel restoration** (+50% or more)
- **Extra life** (guaranteed)
- **Progress checkpoint**
- **Increased difficulty for next section**

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I save my progress?**
A: High scores and coins are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. No account needed!

**Q: Can I play offline?**
A: Yes! Once loaded, the game works completely offline.

**Q: Does the Wingman cost coins every time?**
A: Yes, 5 coins per game session. But you earn them back quickly!

**Q: What happens when I run out of lives?**
A: Game over! Your score is saved if it's a high score, and you return to the menu.

**Q: Can I pause the game?**
A: Yes, press 'P' on desktop or tap the pause button on mobile.

**Q: Are there different difficulty settings?**
A: The game has adaptive difficulty that increases as you play.

**Q: Do power-ups stack?**
A: No, collecting a new power-up replaces the current one (except shield).

**Q: How many bosses are there?**
A: Bosses appear periodically throughout endless gameplay with increasing difficulty.
