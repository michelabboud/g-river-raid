# Game Guide - River Raid Remastered

Welcome, pilot! Your mission is to fly up the River of No Return, destroying enemy installments and bridges while managing your fuel supply.

## Controls

- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD**: 
  - **Left/Right**: Move the jet laterally.
  - **Up**: Accelerate (Fly faster).
  - **Down**: Decelerate (Fly slower).
- **Spacebar**: Fire weapons.
- **P**: Pause / Resume game.

## HUD (Heads Up Display)

- **Score**: Your current mission points.
- **Lives**: Remaining fighter jets.
- **Fuel**: The bar at the bottom center. If it runs out, you crash.
- **Multiplier**: Increases as you fly further without crashing or refueling. Resets on crash or refuel.

## Gameplay Mechanics

### 1. Fuel Management
Your jet consumes fuel constantly. To refuel, fly **over** fuel canisters (`FUEL`). 
- **Pink Canisters**: Restore fuel gradually.
- **Blue Depots**: Instantly refill tank to 100%.

### 2. Combat
- **Enemies**: Shoot helicopters, ships, jets, and tanks for points.
- **Avoid**: Do not collide with river banks, islands, or enemies.
- **Bridges**: Destroy bridges to act as checkpoints and proceed to the next level difficulty.

### 3. Power-Ups
Collect floating icons to upgrade your ship:
- **[W] Spread Shot**: Fires 3 bullets in a fan.
- **[>>>] Rapid Fire**: Increases firing rate.
- **[SPD] Speed Shot**: Bullets travel faster.
- **Shield**: Grants temporary invulnerability.
- **Life**: Extra life (Heart icon).

### 4. Wingman Support
Before starting a game, you can hire a **Wingman** for 5 Bravery Coins.
- The Wingman is an AI drone that flies beside you and shoots enemies automatically.
- Playing with a Wingman doubles your coin earnings (2 coins/min).

### 5. Boss Battles
At the end of each river section, you will face a Boss.
- Dodge its attacks and destroy it to advance.
- Beating a boss restores fuel and grants an extra life.

## Currency
- **Bravery Coins**: Earned by playing time (1 coin per minute of survival).
- Use coins to buy the Wingman upgrade.

Good luck, pilot!
